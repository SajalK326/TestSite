// 05 — Inquiries

Frequently Asked

Who can participate in TOMORROW.EXE? +

Open to all enrolled students of this college only. No external teams. Teams of 2–4. Cross-department and cross-year teams are encouraged.

What's included with registration? +

Registration is required for all events. A participation fee applies only to the Hackathon; all other events are free of charge. Registration forms will be circulated 1 week before the event.

Where will the events take place? +

Each day's events will be held in the department seminar hall or auditorium.

What are the judging criteria for the Hackathon? +

The Hackathon is judged on: Innovation (30%), Technical Execution (30%), Social Impact (20%), and Presentation (20%).

Are there prizes? +

Certificates will be provided for all participants. Trophies and cash prizes will be awarded to the top 3 teams. The Grand Prize is The Ohm Trophy!
